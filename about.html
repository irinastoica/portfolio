About Irina

So who is this girl? A myth, a revolutionist in the coding world or an excellent designer?

A dual citizen of Canada and Romania, from Montreal, Irina is trying to save the world with original and efficient designs. Since childhood, she's been exploiting her drawing talents and little by little she's discovered the real world of design. A few years later she had the chance to discover the Code and although at first it seemed just an array of unfamiliar words, later she was glad to see her wonderful designs on her first website (I will not show it to you, because if you remember clearly the web designs in the early 2000's weren't that great).

A few years later she started to be a true graphic designer, working with customers who were satisfied with her work. Now she appreciates both graphic design and the wonderful world of the web. And guess what? She also speaks French. So send her an email. She'd love to chat.
